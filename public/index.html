RATEBISHOPS

is a place to give and view feedback on classes and teachers
and is run by, created by, and for students at Bishop's.

  • Create an Account
  • Login

Ratebishops is completely anonymous.

Ratebishops is a place to post your thoughts on Bishop's teachers and classes, and your genuine thoughts and reviews should not be censored. Your name and email are not associated with the reviews you submit.

Why use Ratebishops?

On Ratebishops, you can view grades, reviews, and statistics about teachers and classes. Use the site to have a great Bishop's experience and leave feedback for other students.

Minimal information needed

We only require an email to set up your account, but it is only for authorization purposes. We will never send you emails or give emails to third parties.

Created by students

This site was created as part of a programming class at Bishop's, and was made to serve the student community. Ratebishops is 100% free.

Computer Recommended.

Ratebishops is not yet optimized for use on mobile devices yet. Please use it on a computer for best results & full functionality.
